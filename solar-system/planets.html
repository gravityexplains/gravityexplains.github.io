Terrestrial Planets

Rocky planets with solid surfaces.

  • Mercury: Smallest planet, closest to the Sun.
  • Venus: Hottest planet due to a thick carbon dioxide atmosphere.
  • Earth: The only known planet to support life.
  • Mars: Known as the Red Planet, home to the largest volcano.

Giant Planets

Large planets made mostly of gas and ice.

  • Jupiter: Largest planet, famous for the Great Red Spot.
  • Saturn: Known for its stunning ring system.
  • Uranus: Rotates on its side.
  • Neptune: Strongest winds in the Solar System.
