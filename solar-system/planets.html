Terrestrial Planets

Rocky planets with solid surfaces.

  • Mercury: Smallest planet, closest to the Sun.
  • Venus: Thick carbon dioxide atmosphere.
  • Earth: Only known planet with life.
  • Mars: Home to the largest volcano.

Giant Planets

Large planets made mostly of gas and ice.

  • Jupiter: Great Red Spot.
  • Saturn: Famous rings.
  • Uranus: Rotates on its side.
  • Neptune: Strongest winds.
