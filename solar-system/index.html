What is the Solar System?

The Solar System is a gravitationally bound system consisting of the Sun, eight planets, their moons, asteroids, comets, and other small bodies. All these objects orbit the Sun due to its strong gravitational pull.

Main Components

  • The Sun: A massive star at the center of the Solar System.
  • Planets: Eight bodies that orbit the Sun.
  • Moons: Natural satellites orbiting planets.
  • Asteroids: Rocky objects mostly found between Mars and Jupiter.
  • Comets: Icy bodies with glowing tails when near the Sun.
  • Kuiper Belt & Oort Cloud: Regions beyond Neptune.
