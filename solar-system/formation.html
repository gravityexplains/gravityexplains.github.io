Scientists believe the Solar System formed about 4.6 billion years ago from a massive cloud of gas and dust called a nebula.

  1. The nebula collapsed due to gravity.
  2. The Sun formed at the center.
  3. A rotating disk formed around the Sun.
  4. Dust particles collided and stuck together to form planets.
